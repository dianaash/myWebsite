Tribute Page


I lost two of my loved ones in the year 2021 ... my maternal aunt on 6th of April and my paternal grandma on 9th of July. I made this tribute page for respecting them.
My grandma was very kind. At weekends, we always went to her house and she used to make for us delicious dishes, give us sweets and ice-cream, play with my brother and me.
My aunty was very pretty and lovely. She was an artist. She had her own Hairdresser's with lots of employees. She was very successful but she went to coma for two weeks because of a wrong diagnosis by the doctor and passed away.I loved my aunty and grandma soooo much and I didn't want to lose them.I won't forget them because they were very kind to me.
